Get ready for your upcoming KWK camp!

Kode with Klossy is a two-week scholarship program for girls 13-18 to teach them the basics of coding.

What languages will I learn?

For the first week you will learn Ruby on Rails. The second week includes the basics of HTML, CSS, and Sinatra so you can create your very own web application!

What does a typical day look like?

You will arrive at camp by 9 am and start with an improv activity. Throughout the day you will learn new concepts, listen to fun lessons that have labs to go with them, and participate in team building activities. There are breaks and snacks every so often. Lunch is usually around 12.

What do I need to bring? Do I need any prior experience?

If you prefer, you may bring your own laptop. Feel free to bring a notebook and a pen for notes and a purse or small bag for your personal items. No prior coding experience is required here at KWK.

Should I bring my own lunch?

Lunch will be provided. If you have any food sensitivies or allergies, let Kode with Klossy know on the confirmation page.
